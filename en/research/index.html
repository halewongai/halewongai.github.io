How I read a paper (shipping-focused)

  • Find the canonical version: conference vs arXiv vs report (PDF is source of truth).
  • Extract contributions: 1-line problem + 3–6 bullet contributions.
  • Reproduction checklist: env, data, key params, runnable scripts, expected outputs.
  • Experimental comparisons: baselines, metrics, ablations, bottlenecks, limitations.
  • Actionable takeaways: what to adapt for your project and what to watch out for.

Best inputs you can give me

  • Best: direct link to the PDF (USENIX/arXiv/publisher).
  • Good: title + venue/year + authors.
  • Okay: keywords (I will locate the page and fetch the PDF).
